An Evening at The Exchange

1

Arrival & Connection

Enter a beautifully curated space. Select from wines, craft beverages, and hors d'oeuvres as you meet fellow attendees in anticipation of the evening ahead.

2

First Presentation

Our first speaker takes the floor for 25 minutes, sharing their unique perspective, practice, or inquiry.

3

Breakout Dialogue

Small groups discuss what emerged, asking questions and making personal meaning from what you just heard.

4

Second Presentation & Discussion

A second speaker offers a complementary or contrasting lens, followed by another round of intimate dialogue.

5

Integration & Mingling

Closing reflections bring the threads together before open conversation, new connections, and the evening's lingering resonance.
